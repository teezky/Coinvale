Scroll down and the world stays put behind these cards.
This setup usually makes the painted background feel more intentional and less like a large moving wallpaper.
It also separates “world art” from “UI layer” more cleanly, which tends to feel higher quality.
The tradeoff is that on some mobile browsers `background-attachment: fixed` can be inconsistent, so the pseudo-element approach is safer.
